### 40101010 — Diversified Banks
Definition: Large, geographically diverse banks with a national footprint whose revenues are derived 
primarily from conventional banking operations, have significant business activity in retail banking 
and small/medium corporate lending, and provide a diverse range of financial services. Excludes 
Regional Banks, Commercial & Residential Mortgage Finance, and Investment Banking & Brokerage.

NYSE Representative Companies:
  - JPMorgan Chase (JPM) — $4.56T assets
  - Bank of America (BAC) — $2.49T assets
  - Wells Fargo (WFC) — $1.76T assets
  - Citigroup (C) — $1.11T assets

### 40101015 — Regional Banks
Definition: Commercial banks, savings banks and thrifts whose business is derived primarily from 
conventional banking operations such as retail banking, corporate lending and originating various 
residential and commercial mortgage loans funded mainly through deposits. Regional banks tend to 
operate in limited geographic regions.

NYSE Representative Companies:
  - Regions Financial (RF)
  - M&T Bank (MTB)
  - Fifth Third Bancorp (FITB)
  - Synovus Financial (SNV)
  - Western Alliance Bancorporation (WAL)
