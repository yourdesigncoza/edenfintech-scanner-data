## The Thesis

AMN is the **largest healthcare staffing company in the US**, beaten down **85% from its 2022 pandemic peak** ($130+). Revenue collapsed from $5.2B (2022) to $2.7B (2025) as pandemic-era travel nurse demand normalized. The stock is priced for permanent impairment, but the cycle is turning.
